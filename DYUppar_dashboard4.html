Project Performance & Risk Analytics

Why:

With a strong track record of early completions and EPC project execution, risk and performance visibility can support scalability.

Analytical Solution:

  • Build a performance dashboard for all active and completed projects (schedule adherence, budget variance, milestone delays).
  • Integrate risk scoring based on project type, client, location, and resource history.
  • Use historical data to simulate cost/time overruns - input to future project estimations of time and cost.
  • Track utilization of equipment and labor on a project.
  • Track usage of materials – cement, steel, aggregates, etc. – Actual vs. Budget.
  • Track milestone completion and delays – project impact on timeline and cost.
  • Benchmark and compare against projects – highlight best/poor performance and benchmark against industry standards.

KPIs to Track:

  • Schedule Adherence (%)
  • Planned vs. Actual Completion Dates
  • Cost and Time Overruns
  • Equipment Utilization
  • Material Utilization
  • Labor Utilization

Data Needed:

Project plans, actual vs. baseline data, risk registers.
